ZMTLoader for Motor Town Behind the Wheel
A simple and effective mod loader for MT.

How It Works???
ZMTLoader verifies your .pak files, your mods and determines conflicts to check if any fixes have to be applied. Applies the fixes afterwards.
Then starts the game.

Installation!!

Drag the ZMTLoader folder in the Paks folder of the game located at Steam\steamapps\common\Motor Town\MotorTown\Content\Paks
Or you can unzip this archive there

[Paks]/
├── Game.exe
├── MotorTown-Windows.pak
├── OneMod_P.pak
├── AnotherMod_P.pak
├── YetAnotherModMod_P.pak
└── ZMTLoader/         <-- Place the folder here
    ├── ZMTLoader.exe     <-- loader to be ran
    └── ADD_TO_DESKTOP.bat  <-- Double click it to add a shortcut of the loader to your desktop
    └── ...

Usage?
Either from desktop shortcut of the loader after running ADD_TO_DESKTOP.bat or double click ZMTLoader.exe
